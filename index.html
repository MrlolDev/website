Join the Mailing List

Go to our Groups page at groups.google.com/a/elilists.yale.edu/forum/#!forum/ycs and click "Apply to join group". You must be logged in with your Yale account to access the website.

If you have trouble doing that, email felipe.pires@[y].edu and ask for help.


Join YCS

If you are interested in the work we do and would like to help us do more, you should consider joining our team. You can either:

  • come to our weekly dinners (Siliman dining hall, Wednesdays at 6.30pm) and see what we're up to, or
  • send a message to Felipe Pires saying you're interested in helping out.

Looking forward to hearing from you soon.
